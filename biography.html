Biography

Short Bio

    Wenshuai Zhao is currently a senior master student and expected to graduate in Jan. 2020 from the Department of Electronic Engineering, Fudan University, China. At the same time, he obtains double degree from the Department of Future Technology from University of Turku, Finland. Before that, he worked as a Electrical Engineer in the China General Nuclear Power Group during July 2014 to May 2017. He got his B.E. degree, with the major of mechanical design, manufacture and automation in the school of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, Central South University, and graduated as Outstanding Graduate of Hunan Province since June 2014.
